    Twitter Sentimental Analysis

    Abstract

The goal of this project was to predict sentiment for the given Twitter
post using Python. Sentiment Analysis is the process of
`computationally' determining whether a piece of writing is positive,
negative or neutral. It's also known as opinion mining, deriving the
opinion or attitude of a speaker.In this project we only focus on two
major fields positive and negative. We had a complete dataset of 2500000
tweets. One half of tweets are positive labels and the other half are
negative labels Our task was to build a classifier to predict the test
dataset of 10000 tweets.
